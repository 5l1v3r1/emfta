In a nutshell

EMFTA is an Eclipse-based Fault-Tree Analysis (FTA) tool that is built on top of Eclipse and Sirius. Eclipse is the basis for representing models and Sirius provides the necessary functions to represent models.

EMFTA is published under the EPL license.

Dependencies

EMFTA does not have so many dependencies. It mostly relies on Eclipse and EMF (Eclipse Modeling Framework). It also relies on Sirius for the graphical representation.
